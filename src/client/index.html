Wall Setup

(1) Left clicking on empty grid places new wall (with veto count of 0).

(2) Left clicking a wall will undo your veto (if you vetoed).

(3) Right clicking on empty grid does nothing.

(4) Right clicking on wall let's you veto it (only once). If majority of your team vetoes it, wall gets deleted.

Note, can only touch your walls.
